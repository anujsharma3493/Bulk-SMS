## About Bulk SMS

Bulk SMS is a fast and cost-effective communication solution that enables businesses to send thousands of SMS messages simultaneously. It is widely used for promotions, customer notifications, appointment reminders, OTP verification, and transactional messaging.

Unlike many digital communication channels, SMS reaches customers directly on their mobile devices without requiring an internet connection, making it one of the most reliable communication methods.
